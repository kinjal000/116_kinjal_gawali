A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was a famous Indian scientist and the 11th President of India.
He is known as the "Missile Man of India" because of his work in space and defense projects.
I admire him because he was very humble, hardworking, and always motivated students to dream big.
His life teaches us that with dedication and honesty, anything is possible.

Why I Admire Them

  • He inspired millions of students to dream big and work hard.
  • He remained humble and simple even after becoming President.
  • He contributed greatly to India's space and missile programs.
